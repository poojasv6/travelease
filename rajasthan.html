KEY HIGHLIGHTS

| RAJASTHAN

City Palace, Jaipur is a stunning blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture with courtyards, gardens, and museums.

Thar Desert Safari offers camel rides and cultural shows under sunset-lit dunes near Jaisalmer.

Lake Pichola in Udaipur is known for boat rides and scenic views of the floating Lake Palace.

Pushkar Fair is a vibrant cultural event with livestock trade, music, and traditional Rajasthani festivities.

Rajasthani Cuisine features dishes like Dal Baati Churma and Laal Maas, offering royal flavors.
